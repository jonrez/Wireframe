Boxing

Boxing has many of the same benefits as Jiu-Jitsu, but where Jiu-jitsu Incourages humility,

boxing builds pride. The grace and swagger of a boxer is unmatched. The improved balance and

rythm will even change the way you walk. This is only intensified by the understanding that

no untrained opponent can do you harm. This Martial art pairs phenomenally well with Jiu-Jitsu,

as they cover each others deficiencies in technicall abilities as well as in mentallity.

Tennis

The constant practice towards perfection and improvement in tennis invariably reinforces the

immense value of working hard. Discipline also comes into the foreplay, as you will need to

constantly practice and control the pace during a match. Learning from your mistakes in tennis

is imperative, and you can manage mistakes by learning to cope your own abilities

These competitions are great conditioners for the ups and downs associated with a highly ambitious world.
